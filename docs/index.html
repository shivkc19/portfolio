About Me


I am a PMI Certified Project Management Professional (PMP) with over 8 years of experience in leading projects for major Global IT firms. My expertise lies in Agile and Hybrid project development methodologies, where I've effectively managed resources, budgets, and schedules. In the dynamic world of IT, I've had the opportunity to work on various business modules, including order management and invoicing in the Retail industry. I take pride in consistently being recognized for my performance excellence and my contributions to the success of all stakeholders. My commitment to organizational goals is unwavering, and I have experience leading teams of up to ten people, including CSR teams.


My journey began in the picturesque city of Dehradun, known for its academic and research excellence, home to institutions like the Indian Military Academy. Growing up, I had the privilege of living in seven different cities across India, and my career has taken me to three more cities in the north and south of the country.


Over the years, I've accumulated five years of experience in the IT sector. I hold not just one but two post-graduate degrees, with my latest achievement being a degree in Advanced Project Management & Strategic Leadership from Canada.


One of the aspects of my work that I truly relish is engaging with people and understanding their problem-solving approaches, both in life and work.


An experienced Technology Analyst, my track record in the information technology and services industry speaks for itself. I possess a range of skills, including proficiency in Oracle E-Business Suite, Service & Lease Contracts, PL/SQL, Oracle Database, MySQL, Service-Now incident management, and C++..
